# Business Activity Monitoring Activity Wizard
Use the **Business Activity Monitoring Activity Definition** to create, modify, or delete a Business Activity Monitoring (BAM) activity.  
  
|Use this|To do this|  
|--------------|----------------|  
|**New Activity**|Click to define a new business activity. The **New Activity** dialog box opens.|  
|**Edit Activity**|Click to edit the selected business activity. The **Edit Activity** dialog box opens.|  
|**Delete Activity**|Click to delete the selected business activity. In the **Delete Activity** dialog box, click **OK** to confirm the deletion.|
